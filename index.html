Job Requirement-1

Ellenox Pvt Ltd

Job Title : Full Stack Developer


Job Discription : Full-Stack developer to help develop a cross-platform application for iOS, Android and web. The candidate will have full ownership of the front end codebase, partially help with backend, will help establish best practices and design patterns for the application moving forward. The ideal candidate is someone who has an entrepreneurial spirit and an appetite for taking on the challenge of building something from the ground up. Having an interest in finance is a big plus.


Project Budget : 500000+


Primary Skills : 2+ years of professional experience working with Full-stack Development. 4+ years of professional software development experience. Front End Required to have React or Vue.js, HTML5, CSS Angular knowledge would be a plus Good experience with NPM and its usage Back End Working knowledge with MySQL Must have experience integrating with 3rd party APIs Micro Services and Spring boot exp is desired. DevOps: Experienced in GIT/JIRA/Maven Meaningful experience working on large, complex systems.

Number of Current Vacancies -3


Job Type Full-time


Prefered Job Location Remote job (Work from home)


Expected duration of the Project More than 6 months
